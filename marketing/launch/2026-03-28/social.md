## LinkedIn

I've been building in the terminal for years. When it's time to market something, I always had to switch to a browser, open three SaaS tabs, and context-switch into a different mode entirely.

That's what GTMstack fixes.

It's an open source marketing skill pack for Claude Code — 9 specialist commands that live next to your code. `/mlaunch` reads your README and CHANGELOG and generates your full launch kit in under 60 seconds. `/mbrand` infers your brand voice. `/mcmo` writes your positioning brief. The full suite covers copy, email, ads, SEO, and retrospectives.

No accounts. No subscriptions. Install once, use everywhere.

If you use gstack (or just Claude Code), this drops straight into your existing workflow.

github.com/deadjim/GTMstack — MIT licensed, free forever.
